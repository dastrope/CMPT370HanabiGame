5.1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this document is to provide in-depth information about a software version of the cooperative card game Hanabi. This document will outline the features, interfaces, and constraints of the software.

5.1.2 Scope

This software version of Hanabi will be playable for two to five players at once in a real time environment. The players will cooperate through hints to complete fireworks. The game software will include artificial intelligence (AI) to support computer players to along with human players at the same time. The game will be playable online through a dedicated server.
